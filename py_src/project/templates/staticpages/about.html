About

My name is Mathieu Meylan. I work since +12 years in the Internet world, first as System Manager for an ISP and now more then +4 years as a Web developer with Django/Python framework.

Look at my professional capabilities and experience is available in my résumé.

Actually, I focus more in usability & accessibility, but always on online matter. I try to make the Online Communications a reality by using the more occurred tips and tricks of the moment by using powerful open source software!

This is my personal blog. I write here – usually on topics related to front-end development and about my favorite framework Django and also about my personal online usage and hosting experiences – as part of engaging with, and learning from, other developers. But also a place to stay connected with my friends, my family and you.
